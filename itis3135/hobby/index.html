How to Choose Your Platform

Here are 5 things to consider when choosing a gaming platform:

Different Platforms

  • Console Gaming:
    • Popular brands: PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch.
    • Pros: Easy setup, exclusive games, optimized for gaming.
    • Cons: Limited customization and hardware upgrades.
  • PC Gaming:
    • Can be customizable or prebuilt.
    • Pros: Higher graphics quality, upgradeable components, a wider range of games, and access to mods.
    • Cons: Higher initial setup cost, requires regular updates/upgrades.
  • Mobile Gaming:
    • You can game on smartphones or tablets.
    • Pros: Convenience, portability, often cheaper or free games.
    • Cons: Limited in game complexity and control options.

Consider What Games You Want to Play

Some games are exclusive to certain platforms, while others are multiplatform. Consider which games you enjoy and if they are available on the platform you're choosing.

Budget

Consoles are generally affordable upfront, while PC gaming can be more expensive. Mobile gaming is often the cheapest option since most people already own a smartphone.

Portability

If you want to game on the go, consider platforms like the Nintendo Switch or mobile gaming. PCs and other consoles are typically stationary, though gaming laptops offer some portability.

Assess Your Tech Savvy

PC gaming requires more technical knowledge for setup and maintenance, while consoles and mobile devices offer simpler, plug-and-play options for casual gamers.
