Page Design

  • Home Page: Its purpose is to provide a welcoming introduction to the website, highlight key services, and direct users to other pages. The audience/users will be general visitors and potential customers. The content will be a welcome message, featured services and images. There will be no user data entry or validations on this page. The page will contain hyperlinks. Clicking on the hyperlink will take users to different pages.
  • Services Page: Its purpose is to display the full range of services offered, such as tables, chairs, inflatable games, and the mechanical bull. The audience/users will be potential customers interested in renting services. The content will be descriptions and pricing for each service, images of the available rentals. User data entry and validations will not be needed on this page. The page will contain a dropdown and hyperlinks. The dropdown will show the prices, and the hyperlinks will take users to different pages.
  • Photo Gallery Page: Its purpose is to showcase images of past events and services in action. The audience/users include potential clients who want to see visual examples of what's available. The content will be a photo gallery with captions. User data entry and validations will not be needed on this page. The page will contain buttons and hyperlinks. The buttons will be used to allow users to manually scroll through images, and the hyperlinks will take users to different pages.
  • Contact Page: Its purpose is to allow visitors to reach out to the business for inquiries or booking. The audience/users include customers interested in booking services or asking questions. The content will be a contact form and business contact information. Users will be asked to enter data on this page. The data fields will need validations for name, email, and phone number fields. The page will contain a button and hyperlinks. The button will send the message to the business, and the hyperlinks will take users to different pages.
  • FAQ Page: Its purpose is to provide answers to common questions about services, booking, and setup. The audience/users include potential customers looking for more information. The content will be a list of frequently asked questions and answers. User data entry and validation will not be needed on this page. The page will contain dropdowns and hyperlinks. The answer will dropdown when user clicks on the question, and the hyperlinks will take users to different pages.

Dynamic Functionality

Using JavaScript, I will implement dropdowns, a form, and a photo gallery. The dropdown will be included in the Services and FAQ pages because it will dropdown to reveal the price of the services, and the answer to the question. The form will be included in the contact page allowing potential clients to get in contact and book with the business. The photo gallery will be included in the photo gallery page allowing potential clients to see more pictures of the services offered.
